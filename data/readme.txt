📦 Dataset Availability
Due to the large size of the diffusion cascade data (especially time-stamped repost logs), we do not host the raw datasets in this repository. However, all datasets used in this work are publicly available and can be obtained from their original sources as described in our paper:
To evaluate the effectiveness of MSTD, we conduct experiments on three widely used public benchmark datasets: Weibo [1], GossipCop, and PolitiFact [2].

🔗 How to Access Each Dataset
Dataset	Source	Notes
Weibo	Ma et al., CIKM 2016	Contains Chinese fake/real news with time-stamped repost cascades.
GossipCop & PolitiFact	Shu et al., WWW 2019 / FakeNewsNet	English news from entertainment (GossipCop) and politics (PolitiFact), with social engagement logs.
[1] J. Ma, W. Gao, P. Mitra, S. Kwon, B. J. Jansen, K.-F. Wong, and M. Cha, “Detecting rumors from microblogs with recurrent neural networks,” in Proc. IJCAI, 2016.
[2] K. Shu, D. Mahudeswaran, S. Wang, D. Lee, and H. Liu, “FakeNewsNet: A data repository with news content, social context, and dynamic information for studying fake news on social media,” arXiv:2006.07368, 2020.
